FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to register a company in Ireland?+

With HRA, your company is typically formed within 3 business days. We handle all CRO submissions, name checks, and documentation on your behalf.

Are the tax registrations really included at no extra cost?+

Yes — all Business Establishment Packages include complimentary registration for Corporation Tax, VAT, and Payroll Tax (PAYE). Your new venture is ready to operate from the moment it's incorporated.

Do you work with medical professionals?+

Absolutely. We have a specialist package for hospital doctors, GPs, locum doctors, private medical practices, and pharmacy owners. We understand the unique tax and compliance requirements of the medical sector.

Can I work with HRA remotely from anywhere in Ireland?+

Yes. We support clients nationwide with fully remote services via phone, email, and chat. You're never required to come into our Dublin office — though you're always welcome to.

How does fixed-fee pricing work?+

We agree on a clearly defined scope of services upfront and charge a single fixed fee — no hourly billing, no surprise invoices. If your needs change, we'll agree on adjustments transparently before proceeding.
